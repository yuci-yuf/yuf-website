# YUF 2026 — Event Content Master Document

Complete, ready-to-enter content for every event in the Youth United Festival 2026
catalogue. Generated by analysing the seed events in `lib/content.ts` and the
admin **Create / Edit Event** form (`components/admin/EventForm.tsx`).

Use this document to populate each event in the admin panel. Every field below maps
directly to a field in the event form.

---

## How this maps to the event form

| Doc field | Form field | Firestore key | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **ID (slug)** | *(auto from Title)* | doc id | Derived from the title; shown for reference. |
| **Title** | Title (required) | `title` | |
| **Category** | Category (required) | `category` | One of the four seed categories. |
| **Short description** | Description | `description` | 1–2 lines; shown on cards. |
| **Detailed paragraphs** | Detail paragraphs (one per line) | `details[]` | Each paragraph = one line in the textarea. |
| **Rules & guidelines** | Rules / guidelines (one per line) | `rules[]` | Toggle **"This event has rules"** on. |
| **Registration fee** | Registration Fee (₹) | `registrationFee` | Base fee; +2% platform fee +18% GST added at checkout (`lib/pricing.ts`). |
| **Registration limit** | Registration Limit | `registrationLimit` | Blank = unlimited. |
| **Date** | Date | `date` | Stored as a display label, e.g. "14 Feb 2026". |
| **Venue / District** | Venue / District | `venue` / `district` | See location note below. |
| **Display order** | Display order | `order` | Lower shows first within a category. |
| **Status** | Status | `status` | All set to `upcoming` for YUF 2026. |

### ⚠️ Location & venue note
The public registration form (`components/public/RegistrationForm.tsx`) filters events
by **location → venue**:

- **Chennai** → events whose `venue` contains **"Ponneri"**
- **Coimbatore** → events whose `venue` contains **"Coimbatore"**

So an event's `venue` must contain the matching city name to appear under that
location. If an event runs in *both* cities, create it **once per location** (the
slug auto-uniquifies to `-2`), or the org must pick a primary host city. Recommended
venues below use **Ponneri** (Chennai host) and **Coimbatore**. Adjust per the actual
schedule.

### Eligibility baseline (applies to all events unless overridden)
- Open to registered participants of YUF 2026 across the four age bands: 11–13, 14–16, 17–20, 21–23 years.
- A valid government-issued photo ID and the printed/e-mail registration confirmation are mandatory for venue entry.
- Judges'/organising committee decisions are **final and binding**; no appeals.
- Participants must report to the venue at least **30 minutes** before their slot; late arrivals may forfeit their turn.
- Code of conduct: respect, inclusivity, no harassment, no plagiarism — violations lead to disqualification.
- Participants bring their own tools, instruments, props, and accessories unless stated otherwise.

# Arts & Culturals

## 1. Acapella Competition
- **ID:** `acapella` · **Category:** Arts & Culturals · **Order:** 1
- **Suggested fee:** ₹600 (team) · **Limit:** 40 teams · **Date:** 14 Feb 2026 · **Venue:** Ponneri · **District:** Ponneri

**Short description**
Showcase your vocal harmony and group singing skills in an all-voice, no-instruments acapella showdown.

**Detailed paragraphs**
- The Acapella Competition celebrates the purest form of music — the human voice. Groups arrange and perform songs using only vocals: melody, harmony, beatboxing, and vocal percussion, with no musical instruments or backing tracks of any kind. It is a test of blend, timing, pitch control, and creative arrangement.
- Whether you reimagine a film chartbuster, a folk classic, or an original composition, the stage rewards tight harmonies and inventive vocal texture. Groups are encouraged to bring choreographed dynamics and a clear musical identity that makes the audience forget instruments were ever needed.

**Rules & guidelines**
- Teams of 4 to 8 members; every member must be a registered participant.
- Purely vocal — no instruments, no pre-recorded music, no auto-tune or backing tracks.
- Performance duration: 4–6 minutes including entry and exit; exceeding the limit incurs a penalty.
- One pitch pipe or a single starting note from a device is permitted only to set the key.
- Song choice must be appropriate for a family audience; offensive lyrics lead to disqualification.
- Judging criteria: harmony & blend (30%), pitch & rhythm (25%), arrangement & creativity (25%), stage presence (20%).
- Teams must submit their song title and language during registration; provide two members' contact numbers.
- Report to the green room 45 minutes before the scheduled slot for a sound check.

## 14. Tug of War
- **ID:** `tug-of-war` · **Category:** Sports & Games · **Order:** 14
- **Suggested fee:** ₹800 (team) · **Limit:** 24 teams · **Date:** 16 Feb 2026 · **Venue:** Coimbatore · **District:** Coimbatore

A classic team event testing strength, coordination, and teamwork under competitive conditions.

**Detailed paragraphs**
- Tug of War is the festival's ultimate test of collective strength and synchronised effort. Two teams grip a single rope and pull, but victory belongs to the unit that combines raw power with rhythm, footing, and a shared strategy called by their anchor. It is pure, roaring team spirit.
- Run as a knockout tournament with best-of-three pulls, the event is grouped by combined-weight or age band to keep contests even. Beyond muscle, it rewards timing and communication — the loudest cheers of the festival are often heard right here.

- Gloves are permitted; specialised footwear with cleats/spikes is not.
- The rope, markers, and officials are provided by the festival.
