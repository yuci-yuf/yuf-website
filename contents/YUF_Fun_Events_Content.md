# Youth United Festival (YUF)
## Fun Events Handbook

## Treasure Hunt

### About the Event
Treasure Hunt is an exciting team-based adventure that combines problem-solving, observation, teamwork, and strategic thinking. Participants will work together to solve clues, complete challenges, and navigate through various checkpoints in search of the final treasure. This event encourages communication, creativity, and quick decision-making while providing a fun-filled and engaging experience for everyone involved. It is the perfect opportunity to test your team's coordination and enjoy an unforgettable adventure.

### General Guidelines
- Report to the venue at least 30 minutes before the event begins.
- Carry a valid ID card and registration confirmation.
- Teams must be present for the briefing before the hunt starts.
- Follow the instructions provided by the event coordinators.
- Respect the venue, volunteers, and fellow participants.
- Maintain fair play and complete all challenges honestly.

### Rules & Regulations
- Only registered team members are allowed to participate.
- Teams must solve clues in the designated order unless otherwise instructed.
- Tampering with clues, markers, or event materials is strictly prohibited.
- The use of unfair means or outside assistance may result in disqualification.
- Team members must remain together throughout the event unless specified otherwise.
- The first team to successfully complete all tasks and reach the final destination with all requirements fulfilled will be declared the winner.
- The organizers' decisions regarding the event shall be final.
- Registration fees are non-refundable.

## Guess the Song

### About the Event
Guess the Song is an entertaining musical challenge that tests participants' listening skills, memory, and knowledge of music across different genres and languages. Contestants will identify songs based on short audio clips while competing in a lively and enjoyable atmosphere. The event encourages quick thinking, teamwork (where applicable), and a love for music, making it an exciting experience for both participants and the audience.

### General Guidelines
- Report to the venue at least 30 minutes before the event.
- Carry a valid ID card and registration confirmation.
- Follow the seating arrangement and instructions provided by the organizers.
- Maintain silence while audio clips are being played.
- Respect fellow participants, judges, and event coordinators.
- Mobile phones or electronic devices must not be used during the competition.

### Rules & Regulations
- Only registered participants or teams are eligible to compete.
- Participants must answer within the allotted response time.
- Answers must be given only when permitted by the quiz master or event coordinator.
- The use of mobile phones, smart devices, or external assistance is strictly prohibited.
- Any misconduct or unfair practices may result in immediate disqualification.
- Judges' and organizers' decisions shall be final and binding.
- The organizing committee reserves the right to modify the event format if necessary.
- Registration fees are non-refundable.
