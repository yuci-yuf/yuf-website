# Youth United Festival (YUF)
## Athletics Events Handbook

## 100 Metres

### About the Event
The 100 Metres is the ultimate test of speed, explosive power, and athletic performance. As one of the most anticipated track events, it challenges participants to deliver their fastest sprint over a short distance while demonstrating excellent technique, determination, and focus. The competition celebrates the spirit of athletics by encouraging participants to push their limits in a fair and competitive environment, showcasing both individual excellence and sportsmanship.

### General Guidelines
- Report to the venue at least 30 minutes before the scheduled event.
- Carry a valid ID card and registration confirmation.
- Wear appropriate athletic attire and running shoes.
- Complete your warm-up before reporting to the starting area.
- Follow all instructions given by the track officials.
- Maintain discipline and respect fellow participants throughout the competition.

### Rules & Regulations
- Only registered participants are permitted to compete.
- Participants must remain in their assigned lanes throughout the race.
- A false start may result in a warning or disqualification as per the event rules.
- The race will begin only upon the official starter's signal.
- Interfering with another participant during the race is strictly prohibited.
- Officials' decisions regarding timings and results shall be final.
- Registration fees are non-refundable.

## 200 Metres

### About the Event
The 200 Metres event combines speed, endurance, and technical excellence as athletes compete around the curve and into the final straight. Participants will showcase their acceleration, stamina, and racing strategy while striving for the fastest finish. This exciting event promotes healthy competition, discipline, and perseverance, providing athletes with an opportunity to demonstrate their sprinting abilities on the track.

### General Guidelines
- Report to the venue at least 30 minutes before the scheduled race.
- Carry a valid ID card during the event.
- Wear suitable athletic clothing and running shoes.
- Warm up in the designated practice area before the race.
- Follow the instructions of track officials and volunteers.
- Maintain discipline and sportsmanship throughout the competition.

### Rules & Regulations
- Only registered participants are eligible to compete.
- Participants must remain within their allocated lanes until permitted to merge, if applicable.
- False starts will be dealt with according to the competition rules.
- Any obstruction or interference with another athlete may result in disqualification.
- Official race timings and judges' decisions shall be final.
- The organizing committee reserves the right to revise the event schedule if necessary.
- Registration fees are non-refundable.

## 4×100 Metres Relay (Senior Category)

### About the Event
The 4×100 Metres Relay is an exciting team event that combines speed, coordination, and flawless baton exchanges. Teams must work together with precision and effective communication to achieve the fastest overall time. This event celebrates teamwork, trust, and athletic excellence while providing participants with an opportunity to compete in a highly energetic and competitive atmosphere.

### General Guidelines
- Teams must report to the venue at least 30 minutes before the scheduled event.
- Carry valid ID cards and registration confirmation for all team members.
- Wear matching team uniforms wherever possible.
- Ensure all team members attend the pre-race briefing.
- Warm up before entering the competition area.
- Follow all instructions provided by event officials and volunteers.

### Rules & Regulations
- Only registered team members are permitted to participate.
- Baton exchanges must take place within the designated exchange zone.
- Teams must remain in their assigned lanes as required by the competition rules.
- Dropping the baton must be handled according to official relay regulations.
- Unsportsmanlike conduct or rule violations may result in disqualification.
- Judges' decisions regarding the race and results shall be final.
- Registration fees are non-refundable.
