# Youth United Festival (YUF)
## Arts & Cultural Events Handbook

## Battle of Bands

### About the Event
Battle of Bands is a high-energy musical competition that brings together talented bands to showcase their creativity, stage presence, and musical excellence. The event celebrates collaboration, originality, and artistic expression while providing performers with a platform to entertain audiences and demonstrate their passion for music. Participants are encouraged to deliver engaging performances that reflect their unique style and teamwork.

### General Guidelines
- Report to the venue at least 45 minutes before the scheduled performance.
- Carry a valid ID card and registration confirmation.
- Bands must ensure all members are present before the performance.
- Participants should bring personal instruments if required.
- Follow the instructions of the event coordinators and technical team.
- Maintain professionalism and respect fellow performers.

### Rules & Regulations
- Only registered band members are permitted to perform.
- Each band must perform within the allotted time limit.
- Offensive language or inappropriate performances are strictly prohibited.
- Sound checks must be completed within the assigned schedule.
- Judges' decisions shall be final.
- The organizing committee reserves the right to modify the event schedule if required.
- Registration fees are non-refundable.
