# Youth United Festival (YUF)
## Sports Events Handbook

## 1. Cricket

### About the Event
Step onto the field and experience the excitement of competitive cricket at the Youth United Festival. This tournament brings together talented teams from various institutions to showcase their batting, bowling, fielding, and strategic skills in a spirited environment. Beyond the competition, the event encourages teamwork, leadership, discipline, and sportsmanship, making it an ideal platform for players to challenge themselves and celebrate the true spirit of the game.

### General Guidelines
- Report to the venue at least 30 minutes before the scheduled match.
- Carry a valid ID card and registration confirmation.
- Teams must wear appropriate cricket attire and sports shoes.
- Follow the instructions of match officials and organizers at all times.
- Maintain discipline and respect fellow participants, umpires, and volunteers.
- Teams should complete their warm-up before the scheduled start time.

### Rules & Regulations
- Only registered players are permitted to participate.
- Match schedules must be strictly followed.
- Standard cricket rules and the tournament format announced by the organizers will apply.
- Any form of unfair play or misconduct may result in disqualification.
- Umpires' and organizers' decisions shall be final.
- The organizing committee reserves the right to modify fixtures if required.
- Registration fees are non-refundable.
