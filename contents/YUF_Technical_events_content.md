# Youth United Festival (YUF)
## Technical Events Handbook

## Quiz

### About the Event
The Quiz Competition is designed to test participants' knowledge, analytical thinking, and presence of mind across a wide range of subjects. This intellectually stimulating event encourages healthy competition while providing an opportunity for participants to demonstrate their awareness, reasoning abilities, and quick decision-making skills. Whether you're passionate about current affairs, science, technology, history, or general knowledge, the quiz offers an engaging platform to challenge yourself and compete with the best.

### General Guidelines
- Report to the venue at least 30 minutes before the scheduled event.
- Carry a valid ID card and registration confirmation.
- Follow the seating arrangement assigned by the organizers.
- Electronic gadgets or study materials are not permitted unless specified.
- Maintain silence throughout the competition.
- Respect the quiz master, judges, and fellow participants.

### Rules & Regulations
- Only registered participants or teams are eligible to compete.
- The quiz will be conducted according to the format announced by the organizers.
- Participants must answer within the allotted time for each round.
- Any form of malpractice or unfair means will result in immediate disqualification.
- The quiz master's and judges' decisions shall be final.
- The organizing committee reserves the right to modify the quiz format or schedule if necessary.
- Registration fees are non-refundable.

## Debate

### About the Event
The Debate Competition provides participants with an opportunity to express their ideas, defend their viewpoints, and engage in meaningful discussions on contemporary topics. The event promotes critical thinking, effective communication, logical reasoning, and public speaking skills while encouraging participants to respect diverse opinions and present well-structured arguments in a competitive environment.

### General Guidelines
- Report to the venue at least 30 minutes before the event.
- Carry a valid ID card and registration confirmation.
- Participants must adhere to the allotted preparation and speaking time.
- Maintain professionalism and respect opposing viewpoints.
- Follow the instructions provided by the moderators and event officials.
- Ensure that all arguments remain respectful and relevant to the topic.

### Rules & Regulations
- This is strictly an individual event unless otherwise announced.
- Participants must speak only on the assigned topic.
- Personal attacks, offensive language, or inappropriate remarks are strictly prohibited.
- Exceeding the allotted speaking time may attract penalties.
- Judges' decisions regarding scoring and results shall be final.
- The organizing committee reserves the right to modify the event format if required.
- Registration fees are non-refundable.

## Indian Youth Parliament

### About the Event
The Indian Youth Parliament is an interactive platform that encourages participants to understand the functioning of parliamentary democracy while developing leadership, communication, and policy-making skills. Through constructive discussions, debates, and role-play, participants will address important national and social issues, promoting responsible citizenship, teamwork, and informed decision-making in a simulated parliamentary setting.

### General Guidelines
- Report to the venue at least 30 minutes before the session.
- Carry a valid ID card and registration confirmation.
- Participants should be familiar with the assigned role or topic.
- Dress in formal or appropriate attire suitable for the event.
- Maintain decorum and parliamentary etiquette throughout the session.
- Follow all instructions provided by the moderators and organizers.

### Rules & Regulations
- Participants must perform only the role assigned by the organizing committee.
- Discussions should remain relevant to the agenda and parliamentary procedures.
- Personal remarks, offensive language, or disruptive behaviour will not be tolerated.
- Speaking time limits must be strictly followed.
- Moderators' and judges' decisions shall be final.
- The organizing committee reserves the right to modify the session format if necessary.
- Registration fees are non-refundable.

## India's Young Scientist

### About the Event
India's Young Scientist is an innovation-driven competition that encourages participants to present creative ideas, scientific research, and technology-based solutions to real-world challenges. The event aims to inspire curiosity, innovation, and problem-solving while providing young minds with a platform to showcase their projects, research, and scientific thinking before an expert panel.

### General Guidelines
- Report to the venue at least 30 minutes before the presentation.
- Carry a valid ID card and registration confirmation.
- Bring all required presentation materials and project models.
- Ensure presentations are well-prepared and within the allotted time.
- Follow the instructions of judges and event coordinators.
- Maintain professionalism throughout the evaluation process.

### Rules & Regulations
- Only original projects or research work will be accepted.
- Participants must be prepared to explain and defend their work during the evaluation.
- Plagiarism or misrepresentation of work will result in immediate disqualification.
- Presentations must remain within the specified time limit.
- Judges' decisions regarding evaluation and awards shall be final.
- The organizing committee reserves the right to modify the event schedule if required.
- Registration fees are non-refundable.
